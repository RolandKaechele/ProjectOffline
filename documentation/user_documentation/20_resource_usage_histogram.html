Resource Usage Histogram

See at a glance whether your entire team is over- or under-allocated — without inspecting individual resource rows.

Home› Resource Usage Histogram

What the Histogram Shows

The Resource Usage Histogram is a bar chart strip that appears directly below the Team Planner. Each vertical bar represents one calendar day and its height reflects the total allocated hours across all resources on that day compared to the team’s total available capacity.

It answers the question “Is the team as a whole over- or under-allocated?” at a glance — without expanding individual resource rows in the Team Planner or Resource Usage Graph.

Reading the Bars

Bar colourMeaning
GreenUtilisation ≤ 80 % — team is comfortably within capacity
AmberUtilisation 80–100 % — team is near full capacity
RedUtilisation > 100 % — team is over-allocated on this day

A dashed blue line marks the 100 % capacity level so you can see at a glance which bars exceed it.

Capacity Calculation

For each calendar day, the histogram sums the available hours of every resource:

  • Weekends (Saturday and Sunday) count as 0 h capacity.
  • Project-level public holidays (from the project calendar) count as 0 h capacity.
  • Resource vacation exceptions (from a resource’s personal calendar) reduce that resource’s capacity to 0 h on the affected days.
  • All other working days contribute the project’s standard working hours (typically 8 h) per resource.

Allocation Calculation

Each resource assignment’s total work hours are spread evenly across all working days within the assignment span, applying the same exclusion rules as capacity (weekends, public holidays, and that resource’s vacation days). Summary tasks are excluded from allocation.

Example A 40 h task assigned to one resource spanning Mon–Fri contributes 8 h per working day. If Wednesday is a public holiday, the 40 h are spread across the remaining 4 days (10 h each) and Wednesday shows 0 h allocated and 0 h capacity.

Showing and Hiding the Histogram

  1. Open the Format menu.
  2. Click Show Resource Usage Histogram to toggle the strip on or off.

The visibility setting is saved and restored automatically when you reopen the application.

Scroll Synchronisation

The histogram is locked in horizontal alignment with the Team Planner timeline — scrolling the Team Planner left or right also moves the histogram. The day-width (zoom level) is shared too, so zooming the Team Planner rescales the histogram bars at the same time.

Typical Use-Cases

ScenarioWhat to look for
Capacity planningRed bars signal days that need re-scheduling or additional resources
Sprint / milestone preparationLow bars before a deadline reveal slack that can absorb scope creep
Resource levellingSpikes show where work is unevenly distributed across the schedule
Executive overviewThe histogram gives a one-line team-load summary beside the Team Planner
