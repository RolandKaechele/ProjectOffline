Email Ribbon Disabled State

When no SMTP email account is configured (the email_configs.json list is empty) or no active account is selected (the active config name does not match any entry), the three email export actions are automatically greyed-out and non-interactive:

  • Email Gantt SVG — in the Gantt Export popup
  • Email to All Resources — in the Gantt Export popup
  • Email Timeline SVG — in the Timeline Export popup
Hovering over a disabled item shows a tooltip explaining how to configure an account via Report → Email Accounts and Report → Email Config.
The enabled/disabled state is re-evaluated at application startup and whenever the Email Accounts or Email Config dialog closes.
